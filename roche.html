This is an 8-month long end-to-end client project with Roche Molecular Diagnostics. Our project is divided into two main halves: 1) the first 4 months, which we are currently in, are focused on secondary and primary research, as well as some early prototypes, and 2) the latter 4 months are for further ideation and iterative cycles of prototyping and user testing.

To understand the medical oncologist's current ecosystem, workflows, and challenges to identify areas of opportunity that could improve the oncologist's experience when interacting, communicating, and sharing information with their patients.

our plan of attack

Over the last 4 months, we conducted extensive secondary and primary research on the oncology health care team. After synthesizing our initial findings, we identified 3 main areas of opportunity that we could pursue. To further narrow down our focus and validate these opportunity areas, we are continuing to conduct interviews and shadowing oncologists to get a more accurate understanding of the entire ecosystem.

More information will be added as the project continues.
